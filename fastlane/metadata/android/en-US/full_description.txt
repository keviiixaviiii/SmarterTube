SmarterTube is an ad-free YouTube client for Android phones and tablets, built on the SmartTube engine (yuliskov/SmartTube).

It is a companion to SmartTube, not a replacement. SmartTube targets Android TV and set-top boxes; SmarterTube brings a native phone and tablet experience to the same engine. If you are on a TV or set-top device, install the original SmartTube instead.

<b>What SmarterTube adds</b>
• A native portrait UI for phones — Home, Search, Channel, and Settings open in touch-friendly mobile screens instead of a remote-driven TV layout.
• Tablet support — an adaptive multi-column grid that adjusts to screen size and handles rotation.

<b>Built on SmartTube, so you also get</b>
• Ad-free playback, SponsorBlock, and Return YouTube Dislike.
• Background play, picture-in-picture, voice search, and subscriptions.
• No Google account required to browse; sign-in is optional.
• No Google Play Services required.

<b>Compatibility</b>
Android 4.2 and newer, on phones and tablets with a touchscreen. Not available for iPhone or iPad, and not designed for Android TV or set-top boxes.

<b>Permissions</b>
Zero app-declared permissions. Voice search uses the system recognizer (it runs in Google's process, so this app requests no microphone permission).
